❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best golf cart accessories to buy first?

Start with seat covers and a windshield if you don't already have one — these protect your investment and add immediate comfort. An enclosure is next if you ride in rain or cold. Lift kits and custom wheels are the big visual upgrades that most owners eventually do.

Are golf cart accessories universal or brand-specific?

It depends on the accessory. Seat covers, mirrors, and most lighting kits are universal and fit Club Car, EZGO, Yamaha, and Cushman with minor adjustments. Lift kits and enclosures are often brand and model specific — always check compatibility before ordering.

What is the most popular golf cart brand for accessories?

Club Car Precedent and EZGO TXT/RXV have the widest accessory ecosystems due to their popularity. Yamaha Drive and Drive2 are close behind. All four major brands have extensive aftermarket support on Amazon.

How do I know what year my golf cart is?

Most golf carts have a serial number plate on the frame near the driver's side footwell or under the seat. Club Car and EZGO serial numbers encode the model year in the first two or three characters. Search "[your brand] golf cart serial number decoder" for a free lookup chart.

Can I make my golf cart street legal?

Yes — most states allow Low Speed Vehicles (LSVs) on roads posted at 35 MPH or less. Requirements typically include headlights, taillights, turn signals, mirrors, a horn, seat belts, and a speedometer. A complete LED light kit plus mirror set covers most of these requirements.

What size tires fit on a stock golf cart?

Stock golf carts typically run 18x8.5-8 or 18x9-8 tires. With a 3-inch lift kit you can fit up to 22-inch tires. With a 6-inch lift, 23–25 inch tires are possible. Always check your specific cart's clearance before purchasing oversized tires.

What golf cart accessories are best for off-road use?

For off-road, prioritize: a 3–6 inch lift kit, all-terrain tires (20x10-10 or larger), LED light bar, brush guard, and heavy-duty seat covers. Hunters and farmers often add cargo boxes, gun racks, and bed extenders depending on the cart model.
